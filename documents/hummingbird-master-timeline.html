🏗️ Phase Breakdown 9 Phases

Each phase with estimated hours, key tasks, and track classification.

Phase 0 — CAD Finalization

📐 14 hours · ~5 days
  • Complete unified body CAD
  • Payload bay internal mounts
  • Sensor & antenna mount features
  • 4-piece split geometry
  • Export STLs, print settings profile

Phase 1 — Component Sourcing

📦 7.5 hours + 1-4 wk shipping
  • Tier 1: Doodle Labs, ARK PAB, RTK GPS, ARKV6X
  • Tier 2: ESCs, motors, radios, sensors
  • Tier 3: Cameras, props, batteries, wire
  • NEW: CrossLink-NX Eval Board ($132)

Phase 2 — Print & Assembly

🖨️ 12-16 hours · 5-7 days
  • Print 4 body pieces (LW-PLA)
  • Post-processing & dry fit
  • Heat-set inserts & bonding
  • Motor & electronics test fit

Phase 3 — Electronics & Wiring

⚡ 24-26 hours · 8-9 days
  • Mount ARK PAB + Jetson + FC
  • Wire 8 motors to 2× ESCs
  • Power distribution & BEC
  • All sensors, radios, cameras
  • Continuity check & first power-on

Phase 4 — Firmware & Software

💻 22-24 hours · 7-8 days
  • Flash ArduPilot / PX4 on ARKV6X
  • Coaxial quad motor mixing (custom)
  • Jetson: JetPack + ROS2 + TensorRT
  • ROS2-PX4 bridge, sensor validation
  • Radio configuration (3-radio stack)

Phase 5 — System Integration

🔗 8-9 hours · 3 days
  • Full system power-on (all subsystems)
  • End-to-end telemetry verification
  • CG check & weight measurement
  • Multi-radio interference test

Phase 6 — Ground Testing

🧪 12-13 hours · 4-5 days
  • Motor spin tests (no props → props)
  • Thrust measurement on test stand
  • Accelerometer & compass calibration
  • Tethered hover (CRITICAL GATE)

Phase 7 — Flight Testing

🚁 22-24 hours · 7-8 days
  • First free hover
  • PID tuning (rate → attitude)
  • Position hold, altitude hold
  • Autonomous waypoint mission
  • Endurance & failsafe tests

Phase 8 — Debug & Optimize

🔄 16-36 hours · variable
  • Structural repairs / reprints
  • ESC/motor troubleshooting
  • Vibration reduction
  • PID refinement after fixes
  • Documentation updates

⚠️ Risk Register Top 10

R1: Coaxial Quad PID Tuning

Non-standard frame type. No ArduPilot presets. May require 10+ tuning flights. Mitigation: Use autotune, consult ArduPilot community.

R2: Doodle Labs Lead Time

2-4 week shipping. Single most expensive component. Mitigation: Order immediately, test without it initially.

R3: ARK PAB Documentation Gaps

Relatively new product, limited docs. Mitigation: Contact ARK support directly, check Discord.

R4: LW-PLA Frame Strength

Brittle under impact. Mitigation: Spare parts, reinforce with CF-PETG, fly over soft surfaces.

R5: Motor Mount Misalignment

Causes vibration → bad IMU → loss of control. Mitigation: Precision assembly, vibration dampeners.

R6: Weight Exceeds Target

Reduces endurance and T/W ratio. Mitigation: Weigh at Phase 5, remove non-essential sensors initially.

R7: RF Interference (4 Radio Stack)

2.4GHz + 900MHz + LTE + GPS may conflict. Mitigation: Antenna separation, test incrementally.

R8: Hard Crash Destroys Prototype

Weeks to rebuild. Mitigation: Tethered testing first, conservative flying, spare parts.

R9: FPGA Dev Complexity

VHDL/Verilog learning curve. Mitigation: Start with eval board, iterate on sensor bridge firmware.

R10: Simulation-to-Real Gap

Gazebo sim may not capture all real-world dynamics. Mitigation: Early hardware testing, validate assumptions.
